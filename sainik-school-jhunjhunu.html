About Sainik School Jhunjhunu

Sainik School Jhunjhunu, located in the vibrant region of Jhunjhunu, Rajasthan, is a distinguished residential institution established in 2018. The school is dedicated to providing a holistic education that prepares students for entry into the National Defence Academy (NDA) and other prestigious institutions. Affiliated with the CBSE curriculum, Sainik School Jhunjhunu emphasizes academic excellence, leadership training, and physical fitness. Renowned for its modern facilities, the school promotes well-rounded development through various extracurricular activities, sports programs, and the National Cadet Corps (NCC), ensuring that students excel in both academic and military pursuits.

The All India Sainik Schools Entrance Examination (AISSEE) is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admissions into Sainik Schools across India, including Sainik School jhunjhunu.The AISSEE is a pen-and-paper (OMR-based) test comprising multiple-choice questions. For Class VI, the exam includes sections on Mathematics, General Knowledge, Language, and Intelligence. For Class IX, it covers Mathematics, English, Intelligence, General Science, and Social Studies.
