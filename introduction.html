Royal Defence Academy is a premier institute in Jaipur, Rajasthan. It provides coaching for All India Sainik Schools Entrance Examination (AISSEE), Rashtriya Military Schools Common Entrance Test (RMS-CET) in classes 6th and 9th for boys, and Rashtriya Indian Military College (RIMC) Entrance exam for both boys and girls. We admit students in the age group of 7 to 14 years.

We provide Online and Offline coaching classes for these courses, along with proper boarding and schooling facilities.Royal Defence Academy in Jaipur has a record of sending the maximum number of students in the 5 RMS located at Ajmer, Dholpur, Chail, Bangalore, and Belgaum and in all the Sainik Schools spread across India. The only academy which prepares students not only to crack the entrance exams but also to excel in every avenue of studies.

Our main objective

  1. Our primary objective is to raise awareness among the youth about the values of honor, discipline, and integrity in the Defense Services.
  2. Through our training programs, we aim to groom individuals to be competent officers in the defense sector, inculcating in them a deep sense of honor, dignity, and unity.
  3. We aspire to raise awareness among the youth about the core values and ethics of the Defense Services, encouraging them to pursue careers that uphold the principles of honor, dignity, and patriotism.
